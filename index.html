Welcome to Save The Monkeys

Monkeys trapped, save them now!

You must find out the shortest path through this number jungle.

Rules

  • You are at the top-left corner of the jungle, monkeys are trapped at bottom-right.

  • Go down or go right. Rewind your last step if needed.

  • Reach the monkeys before you run out of energy.

  • Greenseer is helpful.

Have fun, hope you enjoy this number game.
