Welcome to Save The Monkeys!

The Game

Monkeys trapped, save them now!

You must find out the shortest path through this number jungle.

Rules

  • You are at the top-left corner of the jungle, monkeys are trapped at bottom-right.

  • Go down or go right. Rewind your last step if needed.

  • Reach the monkeys before you run out of energy.

  • Greenseer is helpful.

Game Modes

There are four modes available: Easy, Normal, Hard and Crazy. Your first 3 games will be in Easy mode, once you finish them, game mode will be changed to Normal.

If you think Normal mode is just a piece of cake, try some Hard or Crazy ones.

In-App Purchases

  • Greenseer Greenseer can show you the right path through the jungle, up to 10 times usage.

Initially you will be given 3 times of Greenseer usages for free, try them! But better not wasted on easy ones:)
